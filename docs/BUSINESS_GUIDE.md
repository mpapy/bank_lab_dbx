## 📊 Dashboard Overview

### Executive Dashboard
**Purpose**: Strategic oversight and key performance indicators
**Users**: C-level executives, senior management
**Update Frequency**: Real-time

#### Key Metrics
- **Total Customers**: Active customer count with growth trends
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: Average CLV with segment breakdown
- **Transaction Volume**: Daily/monthly transaction volumes and trends
- **Risk Indicators**: High-risk customer counts and fraud alert volumes
- **Profitability Metrics**: Revenue per customer, cost-to-serve ratios

### Customer 360 Analytics
**Purpose**: Comprehensive customer relationship management
**Users**: Relationship managers, sales teams, customer service
**Update Frequency**: Near real-time (15-minute refresh)

#### Customer Segments
1. **Affluent Customers** (€80K+ income, 2+ properties)
   - Premium service offerings
   - Investment product opportunities
   - Private banking referrals

2. **Upper Middle Class** (€60K+ income)
   - Mortgage and loan products
   - Investment planning services
   - Premium card offerings

3. **Mass Market** (Standard income levels)
   - Basic banking services
   - Personal loan products
   - Standard card offerings

#### Customer Insights
- **Financial Health Score**: Composite scoring based on savings, income, stability
- **Engagement Level**: Active, Moderate, Low, Inactive based on transaction patterns
- **Product Penetration**: Number and types of products per customer
- **Life Stage Analysis**: Young Adult, Early Career, Mid Career, Peak Career, Pre-Retirement, Retirement

### Fraud Detection Dashboard
**Purpose**: Real-time fraud monitoring and prevention
**Users**: Security operations, fraud analysts, compliance team
**Update Frequency**: Real-time streaming

#### Alert Priorities
1. **CRITICAL** (Immediate Action Required)
   - Fraud score ≥ 70
   - Immediate account blocking recommended
   - 15-minute response SLA

2. **HIGH** (Manual Review Required)
   - Fraud score 40-69
   - Enhanced monitoring activated
   - 1-hour response SLA

3. **MEDIUM** (Enhanced Monitoring)
   - Fraud score 20-39
   - Automated monitoring rules applied
   - 4-hour response SLA

#### Fraud Indicators
- **Amount Anomalies**: Transactions significantly above customer's normal pattern
- **Velocity Alerts**: High-frequency transactions in short time periods
- **Unusual Timing**: Transactions at atypical hours (1 AM - 5 AM)
- **Geographic Anomalies**: Transactions in unusual locations
- **Behavioral Changes**: Sudden changes in spending patterns

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Customer Metrics
- **Customer Acquisition Rate**: New customers per month
- **Customer Retention Rate**: Percentage of customers retained year-over-year
- **Customer Satisfaction Score**: Based on surveys and interactions
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)**: Customer recommendation likelihood
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: Predicted revenue per customer

### Financial Metrics
- **Assets Under Management**: Total customer deposits and investments
- **Loan Portfolio Performance**: Default rates and profitability
- **Revenue Per Customer**: Average revenue generated per customer
- **Cost-to-Serve**: Operational costs per customer relationship
- **Cross-Selling Ratio**: Average products per customer

### Risk Metrics
- **Portfolio Risk Score**: Weighted average risk across all customers
- **Fraud Detection Rate**: Percentage of fraud cases identified
- **False Positive Rate**: Legitimate transactions flagged as fraud
- **Credit Loss Ratio**: Loan defaults as percentage of portfolio
- **Regulatory Compliance Score**: Adherence to regulatory requirements

### Operational Metrics
- **Data Quality Score**: Overall data accuracy and completeness
- **System Availability**: Platform uptime percentage
- **Processing Time**: Average time for data updates
- **Alert Response Time**: Time to respond to critical alerts
- **Report Generation Time**: Time to produce standard reports

## 📋 How to Use the Platform

### Accessing Dashboards
1. **Login**: Use your corporate credentials to access the Databricks workspace
2. **Navigation**: Access dashboards through the "SQL Dashboards" section
3. **Filters**: Use date ranges, customer segments, and geographic filters
4. **Drill-down**: Click on metrics to see detailed breakdowns
5. **Export**: Download reports in PDF or Excel format

### Understanding Data Freshness
- **Real-time Data**: Fraud alerts, transaction monitoring (< 1 minute delay)
- **Near Real-time**: Customer analytics, account balances (15-minute refresh)
- **Batch Updates**: Daily reconciliation, monthly reports (overnight processing)
- **Historical Data**: Available for 7 years for compliance requirements

### Data Quality Indicators
- **Green**: Data quality > 95% - Reliable for decision making
- **Yellow**: Data quality 90-95% - Use with caution
- **Red**: Data quality < 90% - Investigate before using

### Security and Access
- **Role-Based Access**: Data visibility based on your role and responsibilities
- **Geographic Restrictions**: Regional managers see only their region's data
- **Data Masking**: PII is automatically masked based on your access level
- **Audit Trail**: All data access is logged for compliance

## 🚨 Alert Management

### Alert Types and Responses

#### Data Quality Alerts
- **Purpose**: Ensure data reliability for business decisions
- **Response**: Investigate data source issues, contact IT if needed
- **Escalation**: If unresolved in 4 hours, escalate to data engineering team

#### Fraud Alerts
- **Purpose**: Prevent financial losses from fraudulent activities
- **Response**: Immediate investigation, customer contact if needed
- **Escalation**: Critical alerts require immediate action within 15 minutes

#### Performance Alerts
- **Purpose**: Maintain system availability and performance
- **Response**: Monitor for business impact, contact IT for resolution
- **Escalation**: If business operations affected, escalate immediately

#### Compliance Alerts
- **Purpose**: Maintain regulatory compliance
- **Response**: Document issue, implement remediation plan
- **Escalation**: Notify compliance team and senior management
